    Rooms

    The rooms – ranging from Standard Rooms to Villas – feel earthy and private, like a home away from home. Each room features an own private terrace, while the Premium Roof Terrace rooms boast expansive sunset views. Shades of orange and red offset the darker nuances of green and blue, while raw wooden furnishings and tarnished ceramics create a sense of pared back luxury. Décor is simple and chic, complimented with rattan interiors and neutral linens.

    PREMIUM ROOF TERRACE TERRACE - SEA VIEW

    With 48 sqm the Premium Roof Terrace Rooms offers enough space to unwind on your holiday. The highlight of this room type is the spacious private terrace on the roof with a Sea view. They are located on the first floor and feature a comfy king size bed and a sofa, air-condition, free WiFi, SAT-TV, minibar (at extra charge), free safe, coffee machine (at extra charge), bathroom with shower, hairdryer, robes and slippers, direct dial telephone (at extra charge) for max. 2 adults.

The room has either Garden or sea view.
