    POOLSIDE SIP

    WATERMELON COOLER

    Quench your cravings with our signature cocktail, the Watermelon Cooler, handcrafted by our head bartender Michalis Xipas. Made with fresh watermelon from our own garden, this cocktail is best served poolside.

    Ingredients:

    1 piece fresh watermelon (muddled) (70g of watermelon should give you about 40 ml of fresh juice)

    1. 15 ml fresh lime juice
    2. 20 ml simple syrup 1:1
    3. 15 ml Lillet Blanc
    4. 10 ml Maraschino liqueur
    Method:
    1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker and fill with ice cubes.
    2. Shake well and strain into a mug or glass.
    3. Garnish with a piece of fresh watermelon and a sprig of fresh basil.
    Recipe for syrup:
    1. 200ml hot water
    2. 200gr granulated sugar
    3. Dissolve the sugar and pour it into a bottle
    4. Store in the refrigerator
